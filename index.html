About Me

Hi! I'm Samvel, a passionate Machine Learning Researcher with a keen interest in artificial intelligence and data science. I thrive on solving challenging problems and developing innovative solutions that push the boundaries of technology.

Currently, I work as a researcher at YerevaNN while pursuing my bachelor's degree at Yerevan State University (YSU). My primary research focuses on domain generalization and domain shift, exploring how machine learning models can generalize across different domains effectively. I'm deeply fascinated by computer vision, language models, and generative models, areas where I aim to contribute with novel approaches and insights.

I'm driven by the potential of AI to transform industries and solve real-world problems, and I'm excited to continue exploring and contributing to this rapidly evolving field.

Projects

NLP

"Kargin Summarization" is an educational project focused on solving text summarization. In this project, we developed two innovative neural networks trained from scratch using distinct datasets. Additionally, we integrated a complementary approach by fine-tuning them on the BART network architecture.
